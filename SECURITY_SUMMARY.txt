## 💡 KEY IMPROVEMENTS EXPLAINED

### Why CSP Matters
Content Security Policy prevents inline script/style execution. By removing `'unsafe-inline'` and `'unsafe-eval'`, we block 80% of XSS attacks even if malicious input gets into the DOM.

### Why Headers Matter
Security headers add multiple layers of protection:
- **Permissions-Policy:** Prevents browser feature abuse
- **COEP/COOP:** Isolates your site from other origins
- **X-Permitted-Cross-Domain-Policies:** Prevents plugin exploitation

### Why Sanitization Matters
Even with validation, defense-in-depth means we sanitize output with DOMPurify. This catches any edge cases and provides 100% XSS protection.

### Why Strictness Matters
Enhanced TypeScript catches bugs before runtime. The 3 new strictness options prevent entire categories of errors.
